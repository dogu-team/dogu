## Organization ID

<OrganizationMemberPermission prefix="Retrieve organization ID" />

The organization ID is automatically generated when creating an organization and cannot be changed.

1. In the Dogu console, click on "Settings" in the left sidebar of the organization page.
2. Check the organization ID.
   <img
     src="/img/console/organization/organization-id.png"

   />

## Update profile

<OrgnizationOwnerPermission prefix="Updating organization profile" />

1. In the Dogu console, click on "Settings" in the left sidebar of the organization page.
2. Upload a profile image or change the organization name.

## Organization access token {#access-token}

Organization access token is used for [API](/api) and [Test Automation](/test-automation).

<OrganizationAdminPermission prefix="Retrieve and regenerate organization access token" />

:::danger

When regenerating organization access token, the previous token will no longer be valid.

:::

1. In the Dogu console, click on "Settings" in the left sidebar of the organization page.
2. View the existing organization access token or regenerate organization access token.

## Change owner

<OrgnizationOwnerPermission prefix="Changing organization owner" />

:::caution Changing organization owner

The current organization owner will become an `Admin` after the organization owner is changed.

:::

1. In the Dogu console, click on "Settings" in the left sidebar of the organization page.
2. In the "Change organization owner" menu, click on the "Change owner" button.
   <img
     src="/img/console/organization/change-org-owner-menu.png"
     style={{
       width: 600,
       display: 'block',
     }}
   />
3. Select the member you want to set as the new organization owner.
   <img
     src="/img/console/organization/change-org-owner-modal.png"
     style={{
       width: 600,
       display: 'block',
     }}
   />
4. Click the confirmation button to complete the change.

## Remove an organization

<OrgnizationOwnerPermission prefix="Removing an organization" />

:::danger Remove organization

Removing an organization will permanently delete all data associated with the organization. All members belonging to the organization will be removed from the organization.

:::

1. In the Dogu console, click on "Settings" in the left sidebar of the organization page.
2. Click on the "Remove Organization" button.
   <img
     src="/img/console/organization/remove-org-menu.png"
     style={{
       width: 600,
       display: 'block',
     }}
   />

## Leaving an organization

:::info

The organization owner cannot leave the organization. To leave the organization as the organization owner, you need to transfer ownership to another member first.

:::

1. Click on your profile image in the top menu of the Dogu console.
2. Click on the "Organizations" menu.
   <img
     src="/img/console/organization/move-to-my-organizations.png"
     style={{
       width: 600,
       display: 'block',
     }}
   />
3. Find the organization you want to leave in the list and click the menu button(`···`).
4. Click on the "Leave" menu, then confirm by clicking the confirmation button to complete the process.
   <img
     src="/img/console/organization/leave-org-menu.png"
     style={{
       width: 600,
       display: 'block',
     }}
   />
