## Add project members

Project members can be organization members or teams.

<ProjectAdminPermission prefix="Adding project members" />

:::tip Organization Members and Teams

In the project members list, organization members and teams are differentiated based on their **"Type"**.

:::

### Add organization members

1. Go to the project page and click on the "Members" menu in the left sidebar.
2. Click the "Add members" button.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/add-colleague-button.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />
3. Enter the name or email of the member you want to add, then click on the result.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/add-colleague-modal.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />
4. Select the member's permission and click the "Add to Project" button.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/add-colleague-modal-permission.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />

### Add teams

1. Go to the project page and click on the "Members" menu in the left sidebar.
2. Click the "Add teams" button.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/add-team-button.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />
3. Enter the name of the team you want to add, then click on the result.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/add-team-modal.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />
4. Select the team's permission and click the "Add to Project" button.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/add-team-modal-permission.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />

## Change project member's permission

<ProjectAdminPermission prefix="Changing project member's permission" />

1. Go to the project page and click on the "Members" menu in the left sidebar.
2. Find the member whose permission you want to change in the members list, and click the permission select box.
3. Select the permission to complete the change.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/update-member-permission.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />

## Remove project members

<ProjectAdminPermission prefix="Removing project members" />

1. Go to the project page and click on the "Members" menu in the left sidebar.
2. Find the member you want to remove in the members list, and click the menu button(`···`).
3. Click the "Remove" menu, then click confirmation button and complete the removal.
   <img
     src="/img/console/project/delete-member.png"
     style={{ width: 600, display: 'block' }}
   />
